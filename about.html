Our Journey in Scent

Crafting artisanal oil fragrances since 2025

Our Story

Founded in a small atelier, Shifaul Fragrances began as a passion project to capture the essence of nature in scent. From hand-selected botanicals to slow-infusion processes, every bottle tells a story of craftsmanship and care.

As we grew, our commitment to quality and sustainability never wavered. Today, we source ethically, package responsibly, and continue to innovate with new scent experiences.

Mission & Values

Our Mission

To enrich daily life with pure, captivating fragrances that inspire and uplift.

Core Value: Quality

We never compromise—only the finest ingredients make it into our blends.

Core Value: Sustainability

Ethical sourcing, eco-friendly packaging, and transparent practices guide every decision.
